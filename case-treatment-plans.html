The opportunity

A net-new feature none of our competitors had.

This was a true differentiating feature, and the idea was design-led, and I drove it from the start. We noticed providers were recording treatment plans on paper. Clients took them home and lost them. So providers started photographing the paper into the client chart, but a static image can't be used in any meaningful way, and it can't evolve across a client's visits.

What we designed

A plan that grows with the client.

We built an easy system for creating treatment plans, where a provider adds the services they offer and the plan evolves through the course of treatment. Crucially, everything is structured and personalized per client:

  • Capture each client's concerns and goals up front.
  • Record suggested treatments with a recommended frequency and notes, customized for that client.
  • Build a retail plan history that helps clients reach their goals and, just as importantly, supports medspa revenue.
The outcome

Strong, immediate traction.

These are first-week, post-launch numbers. Beyond the metrics, treatment plans became a pillar of the company's new go-to-market positioning around clinically led growth — the story our B2B marketing team now builds the brand around.

What's next · In design

The structured plan data now unlocks personalized outreach.
